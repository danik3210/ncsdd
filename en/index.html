How we work

Local delivery, inclusion and partnership shape every NCSD project.

Local by design

We prioritise solutions that can be produced, maintained and scaled in Ukraine, in close cooperation with local communities.

Inclusive in practice

We account for the needs of internally displaced people, women, older people and persons with disabilities throughout project delivery.

Partnership-driven

We combine Ukrainian expertise with the experience of universities, companies and international donor programmes.

Mission

Who we are

The National Council for Sustainable Development brings together scientists, engineers, civic actors and local government representatives around one goal: to help Ukraine recover in a way that is clean, fair and resilient.

We see energy recovery not as replacing damaged infrastructure like-for-like, but as an opportunity to build safer, lower-cost and more environmentally responsible systems for communities.

To help Ukrainian communities implement practical innovations in renewable energy, waste-to-resource systems and socially inclusive post-war recovery.

Selected initiatives

Thermal storage from construction debris

Sustainable ThermoSolutions: Simple Heat

Simple Heat converts concrete debris from damaged buildings into material for a high-temperature thermal storage system capable of storing heat at up to 1000 C.

Energy, heat and food resilience from waste

BioSolar Nexus

BioSolar Nexus is a modular system that converts household, livestock and food waste into electricity, heat, clean cooking gas and fertiliser.
